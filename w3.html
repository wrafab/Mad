Week 3

Garment, UV printing and Sublimation

Project | Garmint Printer and UV Printer

This week I design three things: a smug cup, a T-shirt and a popsocket. I design the T-shirt with GT-3 Printer; I design the smug cup with a sublimation tools, which include a paper with ink on it and a printer;then, I design a popsocket by the UV printer. Why is Sublimation better than UV printing: 1. I can only upload a small size file on the UV printer instead of a clear picture, which I can upload on a sublimation printer. 2. The color of the product of UV printer is different from the original file(not so obvious with sublimation)
